What is Reiki?

The Art of Energy Healing

Reiki (pronounced "ray-key") is a gentle yet powerful form of energy healing that originated in Japan. The word "Reiki" combines "rei" (universal) and "ki" (life force energy), representing the universal life force energy that flows through all living things.

The Reiki Experience

During a Reiki session, the practitioner serves as a channel for universal life force energy, allowing it to flow through their hands to the recipient. This energy works intelligently, flowing to where it's needed most in your body and energy field.

The Benefits

Reiki promotes balance and harmony on all levels - physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual. It helps to:

  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Promote deep relaxation
  • Support natural healing
  • Restore emotional balance
  • Enhance spiritual awareness
  • Accelerate the body's self-healing abilities

Our Practice

At Reiki at Hope Cottage, we create a sacred space where healing and transformation can occur. Our practice combines traditional Reiki healing with the calming presence of nature, offering a holistic approach to wellness and balance.

Each session is a unique journey, tailored to your individual needs and guided by the universal life force energy. We believe in the body's natural ability to heal itself and use Reiki as a gentle catalyst for this process.
