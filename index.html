What is Reiki?

The Art of Energy Healing

Reiki (霊気) is a gentle yet powerful form of energy healing that originated in Japan. The word combines "rei" (universal) and "ki" (life force energy), representing the universal life force energy that flows through all living things.

The Reiki Experience

During a Reiki session, the practitioner serves as a channel for universal life force energy, allowing it to flow through their hands to the recipient. This energy works intelligently, flowing to where it's needed most in your body and energy field.
