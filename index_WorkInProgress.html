What is Reiki?

About Reiki

Reiki (霊気) is a spiritual and energy flow complementary paradise, that originated in Japan around the early 20th Century. It involves a practitioner channeling energy through their hands, either lightly touching or placing on the recipient's seven energy centers, "Chakras", with the positive intention of promoting balance and well-being.

While Reiki is not a substitute for medical treatment, many people use it as a complementary therapy to their wellness routines.

Core Principles of Reiki

  • Energy flow Reiki, is based on the concept of "Ki" or "Chi" meaning life force energy that flows through all living things. By channeling this energy, you can help remove blockages from physical and emotional traumas, plus support the body's natural healing.
  • Holistic approach Reiki emphasises the connection between mind, body and spirit, to bring about balance and overall harmony and contentment.

Benefits of Reiki

Some of the benefits of Reiki include the client experiencing a sense of deep relaxation, emotional clarity and reduced stress. Many clients find it very helpful to enhance self-awareness, personal boundaries and overall self-wellbeing.

Reiki Session

40-60 minutes

Initial Consultation

We start with a brief conversation where I might ask about your intentions for the session or any particular areas of the body's energy centers you would like me to focus on. This just gives me an idea of where to direct energy.

Treatment

You will remain fully clothed in comfortable clothing, you will lie down on a treatment table, covered with blankets to keep you warm and secure. I like to play music in the background and use essential oils to create a calming atmosphere. During the session I will use hand positions, either lightly touching, resting or hovering just above your energy centers "Chakras". Each position is held for several minutes. Many clients feel sensations of warmth, tingling and deep relaxation.

After the Session

After the treatment, I will give you time to process and absorb the energy given. We can discuss your experience and emotions if you would like to, but absolutely no pressure to do this!

Rahanni

I am also a Rahanni Practitioner.

Rahanni means One Heart—a connection through our heart Chakras.

Rahanni is a gentle healing modality with a special connection to the heart Chakra. It is similar to Reiki, but emphasizes unity, oneness, and emotional healing.

This therapy is directed through the heart center, helping to bring inner peace, open you to love and compassion, and gently release fear and negative thinking.

Rahanni also encourages a deeper connection with angelic beings and your own guardian angels, vibrating on a pure ray of light—love and compassion.
